Waterproofing

Waterproofing is a fundamental aspect of creating a building envelope. Many types of waterproof membrane systems are possible and CTT truly wants to bring the best for clients and avoid:
- Water seepage,
- Mold growth to air quality issues,
- Water damage to building structure.

Major problems occur in the construction industry when improper products are used for the wrong application.

Masonry - Structural

The evolution of masonry structures has resulted in development of not only more robust materials over the years but also more robust technology. The masonry structures are well-known for their simplicity in construction and economy compared to steel and reinforced concrete structures.
